**Failover Process:**
1. Route 53 health checks detect primary region failure
2. Automatic DNS failover to DR region (<30 seconds)
3. Lambda functions in DR region activated
4. RDS read replica promoted to primary (<5 minutes)
5. S3 data accessible immediately (versioned, replicated)

**Recovery Metrics:**
- **RTO (Recovery Time Objective)**: <1 hour
- **RPO (Recovery Point Objective)**: <15 minutes
- **Data Durability**: 99.999999999% (11 nines)

## ğŸ—ï¸ Design Decisions

### **1. Why Serverless (Lambda + Fargate)?**

**Decision**: Use serverless compute instead of EC2 instances

**Rationale**:
- âœ… **Cost**: 65% cheaper (pay per use vs. always-on servers)
- âœ… **Scalability**: Auto-scale from 0 to 1000s of executions
- âœ… **Security**: Isolated execution environments, no OS patching
- âœ… **Compliance**: Managed infrastructure reduces audit scope
- âœ… **Availability**: AWS handles infrastructure failures

**Trade-offs**:
- âŒ Cold start latency (<1s, acceptable for this use case)
- âŒ Execution time limits (15 min Lambda, unlimited Fargate)

---

### **2. Why Step Functions for Orchestration?**

**Decision**: Use Step Functions instead of custom workflow engine

**Rationale**:
- âœ… **Visual Workflow**: Easy to understand and debug
- âœ… **Error Handling**: Built-in retry logic and error paths
- âœ… **State Management**: Automatic state persistence
- âœ… **Integration**: Native integration with 200+ AWS services
- âœ… **Audit Trail**: Every execution logged and traceable

**Trade-offs**:
- âŒ Cost at scale (but still <$0.01 per execution)

---

### **3. Why Multi-AZ but Not Multi-Region Active-Active?**

**Decision**: Active-active in multiple AZs, warm standby in DR region

**Rationale**:
- âœ… **Cost**: 40% cheaper than full active-active multi-region
- âœ… **Complexity**: Simpler to manage and reason about
- âœ… **Compliance**: Data residency requirements favor single region
- âœ… **RTO/RPO**: <1 hour RTO, <15 min RPO meets requirements

**Trade-offs**:
- âŒ Manual failover required (vs. automatic in active-active)

---

### **4. Why Comprehend Medical vs. Custom NLP Model?**

**Decision**: Use AWS Comprehend Medical instead of training custom model

**Rationale**:
- âœ… **Accuracy**: 95%+ out-of-box, pre-trained on medical corpus
- âœ… **Time to Market**: Immediate deployment vs. months of training
- âœ… **Maintenance**: AWS handles model updates and improvements
- âœ… **HIPAA Compliant**: Covered under BAA
- âœ… **Cost**: $0.001 per unit vs. $10K+ ML engineering costs

**Trade-offs**:
- âŒ Less customization (but covers 47+ entity types)

---

### **5. Why DynamoDB for Audit Logs Instead of RDS?**

**Decision**: DynamoDB for write-heavy audit trail, RDS for consent management

**Rationale**:
- âœ… **Write Performance**: 100K+ writes/sec vs. 10K for RDS
- âœ… **Scalability**: Seamless auto-scaling, no downtime
- âœ… **Cost**: Pay per request, cheaper for write-heavy workloads
- âœ… **Durability**: Multi-AZ replication by default

**Trade-offs**:
- âŒ Limited query patterns (but audit queries are simple)
